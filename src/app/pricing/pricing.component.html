Common Questions
Is there an annual contract?
No. You pay month to month after your 30 day free trial. We believe you should only pay for the things you actually use and receive value from. We’ll never impose contracts on our clients.
How many users can join?
Any number. There’s always a single account owner, who can assign as many admins as they’d like, and then an unlimited number of users download the Compliancechimp app to gain access to all of the features and tools.
Can users login via desktop (web)? Or only Mobile?
User features are all mobile dependent. Capturing worksite logs, accessing training, responding to simple surveys, reporting or investigating an injury, and all other user features are powered by the app which is available for free from the iTunes or Google Play store. Owners and admins are the only ones who access the back office via the web.
Is there a limit to the amount of pictures, logs, or self-inspections?
No. $99/month covers the cost of storing and retrieving pictures, worksite logs, self-inspection and incident reports, plus real-time location tracking and communication...everything.
What languages is Compliancechimp offered in?
English, and Spanish is becoming more complete every day. If you see translation issues, or have language requests, please drop us a note at support@compliancechimp.com.

Of course. And we’ll even refund your current month.
