How it works.
Compliancechimp wraps its arms around the OSHA compliance, training, record-keeping, and worksite related activities that enable a safer workplace and protect your business in the event of an injury, inspection, audit, claim, or litigation. As an added bonus, it even tracks employee time for payroll and allows you see employee activity and location, real-time. An explanation of the setup and our entire feature list is below:
The Setup.
It's easy
