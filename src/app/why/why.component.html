Why Minute?
Minute enables a safer and more compliant workplace, while also capturing the historical record needed to protect your business in the event of an accident or client litigation. We handle the painstaking detail in four key areas, which frees you up to confidently grow your business.
Worksite logs
Worksite logs become a daily reality with Minute. Any employee can log anything, realtime or anytime, captured with text and pictures within the Minute app. Time and location are also logged. Capture the entire historical record of your projects, happenings, and any incidents. Worksite logs, when combined with time, location, and training surveys, create a robust record needed in the event of an accident or inspection.
Team safety training
Training content is available 24/7, and surveys are delivered to your team’s mobile phone to ensure you have a record of training received, from every employee. This enables a safer workplace and ensures leadership and teams stay on the exact same page. The survey responses create the historical record of training received, which is a critical OSHA precaution.
OSHA Compliance with Self-inspection
Minute contains OSHA’s self-inspection content and a wildly useful self-inspection wizard to tailor the OSHA questionnaire to your workspace. Take the assessment often and have the results stored forever. In the words of OSHA, “Self-inspection is essential if you are to know where probable hazards exist and whether they are under control.” We agree, and minute makes it easy.
Time cards and locations
Take the hassle out of keeping time. With Minute, time is recorded beautifully on every user’s mobile phone, allowing you to run a simple report each pay period. All records are kept historically. You’ll save time and money every pay period, with Minute.
