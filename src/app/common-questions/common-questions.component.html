How is Compliancechimp free when other solutions cost hundreds or more every month?
Compliancechimp is indeed free. It’s not a gimmick. We make money by offering optional printed monthly and annual compliance histories for any business on the platform. These are physical printed records of all activity recorded in Compliancechimp. We call them Chimp Books. You can see at a glance, offline, all self-inspections, worksite logs, injury and near miss reports, employee surveys, and more. You can of course see this exact same information within the software, all the time. But it’s surprisingly nice to have a printed record which you can make available to employees (which is one of OSHA’s requirements), OSHA inspectors, higher ups in the company, and the like. It's a physical compliment to a digital solution. Monthly, these printed records cost $59 and you can set them up to arrive like clockwork. Or, get a single consolidated yearly record for $499. Or, just use the software for free forever!
Is there an annual contract?
No, of course not. Use Compliancechimp as long as it is useful to you (which we hope is forever).
Is a credit card required?
No. If you choose to purchase a printed compliance history (a Chimp Book), you'll enter payment details at that time. But otherwise, no payment information is ever required.
Can users login via desktop (web)? Or only Mobile?
Worker features are all powered by our mobile app. Capturing worksite logs, accessing training, responding to surveys, reporting or investigating an injury or near miss, using the time tracking features, and all other worker features are part of the mobile app which is available for free from the iTunes or Google Play store. This puts the power of our software in the hands of every worker which is a game changer. Owners and admins are the only ones who access the back office via the web and that should be done on a desktop or laptop. The back office is powerful and is best experienced with the larger viewing area provided by desktop or laptop.
How many users can join?
Any number. There’s always a single account owner, who can assign as many admins as they’d like, and then an unlimited number of users download the Compliancechimp app to gain access to all of the features and tools from their mobile phone. There's no limit to team size.
Is there a limit to the amount of pictures, logs, surveys, or self-inspections?
No. Activity is unlimited for every feature.
